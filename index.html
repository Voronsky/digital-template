Catch them Dogs!

  • High Concept: Try your best to catch them dogs!
  • Player Experience Goals: What experience do you want players to have when playing your game?
  • Genre: A single sentence that places the game within a game genre or a hybrid of genres.
  • Game play: "Catch Them Dogs!" is a semi-side scrolling game. Overall, you play as a character whose task in his world is to catch dogs. The game requires that players time their jumps to avoid pesky rabbits that tend to kill the player and reset the game. There is also a timer to keep an eye out for, and if the timer hits zero, the game is instantly reset. The characters and somewhat layout is similar to the Phaser game "Starstruck" but with addon of simple elements such as collectable objects and enemies.
  • Prototype goal: Time efficiency and speed. That is, are players quick with their movements to maximize their point gain before time runs out.
  • Features: The unique features is slightly moveable AI. There is an enemy that jumps and moves at random and if the enemy touches the player, the player instantly dies. This adds a bit more of complexity to the concept of "catching the dogs"
